# Sample Q&A — Groww MF FAQ Assistant
## Mirae Asset Mutual Fund · Large Cap · Flexi Cap · ELSS Tax Saver

---

### Q1: What is the exit load for Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund?

**A:** Exit load for Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund is 1% if redeemed within 1 year (365 days) from the date of allotment. After 1 year, the exit load is NIL.

🔗 **Source:** https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/docs/default-source/fund-detail-exitload/entry-exit-load-(1).pdf
*Last updated from sources: Feb 2025*

---

### Q2: What is the ELSS lock-in period?

**A:** Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund has a mandatory statutory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of allotment. For SIP investments, each instalment is locked in for 3 years from its respective investment date.

🔗 **Source:** https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/mutual-fund-scheme/equity-fund/mirae-asset-elss-tax-saver-fund
*Last updated from sources: Feb 2025*

---

### Q3: What is the minimum SIP for Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund?

**A:** The minimum SIP amount for Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund is ₹500 per month. The minimum lump-sum investment is also ₹500.

🔗 **Source:** https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/mutual-fund-scheme/equity-fund/mirae-asset-elss-tax-saver-fund
*Last updated from sources: Feb 2025*

---

### Q4: What is the benchmark for Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund?

**A:** The benchmark for Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund is Nifty 100 TRI (Total Return Index). This was chosen because the fund predominantly invests in the Top 100 companies by market capitalization, which aligns with the Nifty 100 index composition.

🔗 **Source:** https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/docs/default-source/sid/sid-mirae-asset-largecap-fund.pdf
*Last updated from sources: Feb 2025*

### Q5: What is the riskometer rating for Mirae Asset Flexi Cap Fund?

**A:** The risk profile of Mirae Asset Flexi Cap Fund is rated 'Very High' on the SEBI Riskometer. This reflects the fund's dynamic equity allocation across large cap, mid cap, and small cap stocks.

🔗 **Source:** https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/mutual-fund-scheme/equity-fund/mirae-asset-flexi-cap-fund

### Q6: How do I download my capital gains statement on Groww?

**A:** To download your Capital Gains Statement on Groww: Log in → Go to 'Reports' → Select 'Capital Gains Statement' → Choose the financial year → Download as PDF or Excel. You can also get a consolidated statement from CAMS at camsonline.com.

🔗 **Source:** https://groww.in/p/tax/capital-gains-statement
*Last updated from sources: Feb 2025*

### Q7: What tax benefit does the Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund offer?

**A:** Investments in Mirae Asset ELSS Tax Saver Fund qualify for a tax deduction of up to ₹1,50,000 per financial year under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961. Investors in the 30% tax bracket can save up to ₹46,800 in income tax (under the old tax regime).

🔗 **Source:** https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/mutual-fund-scheme/equity-fund/mirae-asset-elss-tax-saver-fund
*Last updated from sources: Feb 2025*

---

### Q8: What is the minimum SIP for Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund?

**A:** The minimum SIP amount for Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund is ₹1,000 per month (in multiples of ₹1 thereafter). The minimum lump-sum purchase amount is ₹5,000.

🔗 **Source:** https://www.miraeassetmf.co.in/mutual-fund-scheme/equity-fund/mirae-asset-large-cap-fund
*Last updated from sources: Feb 2025*
